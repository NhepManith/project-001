  • Welcome To Wash-Project

    Our project aims to improve hand hygiene practices and promote cleanliness among students in rural areas. The objective is to ensure that students have access to proper handwashing facilities, knowledge about effective hand hygiene practices, and a sanitized learning environment.

    Our project objective is to improve hand hygiene and cleanliness among students in rural areas, ensuring access to proper handwashing facilities and promoting hygiene practices. Through educational programs, infrastructure development, hygiene kit distribution, and community involvement, we aim to create a healthier and sanitized learning environment for students, reducing illness-related absences and empowering them with essential skills for disease prevention.
